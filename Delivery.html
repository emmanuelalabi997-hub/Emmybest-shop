FAQ

Can I pay on delivery?

Yes, you can absolutely pay on delivery! We offer a cash-on-delivery option so you can conveniently pay for your order when it arrives right at your doorstep.

Can I return a product ?

Yes, you can absolutely return a product! If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help. To start your return process, simply send us a direct message on WhatsApp, and our team will guide you through the next steps. Please note that returns must be initiated within 3 days of receiving your product, as we cannot accept returns after this period.

How can i track my order?

Tracking your order is quick and easy! You can track your package directly through WhatsApp by simply sending us a message with your order details. Once you reach out, our team will look up your status and respond with your tracking information right away.
